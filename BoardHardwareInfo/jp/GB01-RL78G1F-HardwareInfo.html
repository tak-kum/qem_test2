RL78/G1F Motor Driver Board GB01

P13130-D1-003
  • 幅広い電圧レンジと大電流に対応 (Input voltage of 12 to 50V, Peak current of 30A)
  • 様々なBLDCモータ制御が実現可能(3-shunt, 1-shunt, Encoder, Hall, Sensorless, FOC, Trapezoidal)
  • ツール用通信ボード(またはICS)を接続してRenesas Motor Workbenchが利用可能

製品仕様

    • Input Voltage: DC 12 to 50V
    • Input Current Limit: 30[A]＠ shunt 10mΩ
    • Current Detection Method: 3-shunt and 1-shunt (DC Link)
    • Target Motor: BLDC Motor
    • Dead Time: more than 1µs
    • Heat dissipation: Natural air cooling with heat sink
